Events

Below are the sporting events and tournaments offered by TitleTown Events! Please take a look around and let us know if you have any questions.


Basketball

The Big Island Holiday Classic is a tournament conducted in Hilo, Hawai'i over two separate occasions. Tournaments are played the Friday and Saturday after Thanksgiving and the week before Christmas.

Softball

The Ulili Softball Invitational is ran in Honolulu, Hawai'i during the first two weeks of February (D2 Only) and March (D3 and NAIA). We partner with the Hawai'i Softball Foundation and Fastpitch Hawai'i for this event.

Soccer

We offer soccer players a Spring Break excursion held in Honolulu, Hawai'i in the month of March. Your team will play at least three teams in Hawai'i: University of Hawai'i, Hawai'i Pacific University, and Chaminade University. You will also be able to participate in extra activities around the island.
